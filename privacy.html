Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Cognora Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company details provided voluntarily via our contact forms.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via Google Analytics).
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services (Web Development, App Development, etc.).
  • To communicate with you regarding quotes, project updates, or support.
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.
  • To send periodic newsletters (only if you have opted in).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with our business partners and advertisers.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at info@cognoratech.com.
